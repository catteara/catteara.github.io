Preston Potato Problems

Now you maybe thinking to yourself "what kind of news do they have in Preston?" The correct answer would in fact be no news, but this is Idaho so we will just write something about potatoes and you will believe us. Potato crops are at an all time low this year in Preston, which leaves reporter and scientists everywhere to believe there will be a lot of broken hearts this year when Thanksgiving comes around. That's right folks, there will not be enough crops this year for everyone's favorite dish, mashed potatoes. While we understand this news is heart breaking we encourage everyone one to keep going and if you need someone to talk to about your distress about the situation please contact (098) 765-4321.
