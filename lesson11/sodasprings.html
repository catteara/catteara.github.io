Days of May Celebration

With only a couple months left to prepare for the Days of May Celebration, you can already feel the anticipation and excitement build up. Everyone is looking forward to doing their part this year after it had being cancelled the previous year because of a global pandemic. Nothing fixes up a contagious virus like a town of people getting together and dancing through fountains of soda. I think I speak for all of us when I say that its a sticky tradition that has been missed.
