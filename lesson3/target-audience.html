Target Audience

This website can be an effective resource for many people from residents to travelers, we hope to attract the attention of 40-60 females that live in the area and 40-60 females interested in visiting.

Personas

Granny Smith. 60 years old. Wants to use her phone more to keep up with her grand children. Has lived in the area entire life.

Cheryl Lemmins. 45 years old.

Scenarios

Granny Smith is wanting to go walking around her neighborhood to get out of her house in Preston for a little while. She's going to check Weather Weekly to ensure her plan for tomorrow won't be disrupted by a cold front because she doesn't want to worry about slipping on ice during her walk.

Cheryl is wanting to plan a fun getaway for her and her husband's 25th wedding anniversary. They both love the snow and are still looking for adventure together. Cheryl could use Website Weekly to make sure the there will be snow, but that there won't be too much that they would be stuck inside all vacation.
