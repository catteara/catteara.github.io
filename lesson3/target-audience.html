Target Audience

This website can be an effective resource for many people from residents to travelers, we hope to attract the attention of 40-60 females that live in the area and 20-60 females interested in visiting.

Personas

Granny Smith. Female. 60 years old. Wants to use her phone more to keep up with her grand children. Has lived in the area entire life.

Cheryl Lemmins. Female. 45 years old. Loves to travel, but likes to be prepared. Prefers computer for larger text size.

Scenarios

Granny Smith is wanting to go walking around her neighborhood to get out of her house in Preston for a little while. She's going to check Weather Weekly to ensure her plan for tomorrow won't be disrupted by a cold front because she doesn't want to worry about slipping on ice during her walk.

Cheryl is wanting to plan a fun getaway for her and her husband's 25th wedding anniversary. They both love the snow and are still looking for adventure together. Cheryl could use Website Weekly to make sure the there will be snow, but that there won't be too much that they would be stuck inside all vacation.

David wants to show his sister Lily what Soda Springs looks like in the summer. He sends Lily to Weather Weekly's Gallery because he knows she can find images there.

Grace is a recently single mother of two and doesn't quite have her emergency storage where she would like to, especially when she hears about an incoming storm. She decides to check Weather Weekly to see what time the storm is blowing in and if she needs to wake up her kids now to go get supplies or if she can wait until their nap is over to go.

Kelly just started school in Rexburg and keeps hearing about a place called Fish Haven and she wants to know more about what everyones talking about. After a quick search trying to find pictures, she is able to find Weather Weekly's Gallery and loves the scenery. She decides that she wants to know what the weather is like because she is interested in visiting.
