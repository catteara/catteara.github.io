Color Scheme

I plan to use...

Rich Black FOGRA 39, also known as #0C0C0C, for my logo and body text. Gainsboro is a grey color, also known as #DBE0E6, for the background of the main portion of my page and for text on darker backgrounds. As well as for the background of my logo. Space Cadet is a darker blue, also known as #223559, for my header, nav, and footer. Purple Navy, also known as #5D5988, is my darker accent color. I plan to use it for the :hover in my navigation and for any web links on lighter backgrounds. Celeste is a pale blue, also known as #ADFFFA, it will be used for links on darker backgrounds and for other accents as needed.
